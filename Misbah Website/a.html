Online banking has revolutionized the way people manage their finances, offering unparalleled convenience and accessibility. Customers can perform a wide range of banking activities from the comfort of their homes or on the go using computers, smartphones, or tablets. Services include checking account balances, transferring funds, paying bills, and even applying for loans or credit cards. The adoption of online banking has surged, driven by advancements in technology and increased internet penetration. Security features such as encryption, two-factor authentication, and biometric verification have significantly improved, ensuring safer transactions. Additionally, online banking reduces the need for physical branch visits, saving time and resources for both customers and banks. This shift has also enabled financial institutions to offer more personalized services through data analytics, enhancing customer experience. Despite the benefits, challenges such as cybersecurity threats and digital literacy remain, requiring continuous innovation and education to fully realize the potential of online banking.
